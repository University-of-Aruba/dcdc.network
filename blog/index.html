A seed planted: the DCDC Network meets IPA

On 16 March, the DCDC Network visited Instituto Pedagogico Arubano for a "sip & learn" dialogue on open research. Participants raised questions that many researchers in the region hold but rarely voice, and the conversation that followed was exactly the kind the network was built to have.
