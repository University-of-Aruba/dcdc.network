What we do

The DCDC Network aims to break down data silos between Caribbean research institutions and foster open, FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable), and collaborative data practices.

Through the network, we:

  • Build digital competence via peer-to-peer exchange and training programs
  • Facilitate strategic partnerships across islands and sectors
  • Connect professionals with shared interests in open science and data management
  • Develop a collective vision for knowledge sharing in the Dutch Caribbean

Whether you're deeply experienced in open science or just beginning to explore these topics, you're welcome here.
