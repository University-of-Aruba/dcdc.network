Welcome to the first DCDC Network newsletter

This is the first edition of a regular update from the network. The aim is simple: keep members across Aruba, Curaçao, St. Martin, and IPA in the loop on what is happening, who is involved, and where to plug in. Each issue will share recent stories, spotlight a member of the network, and point to ways to contribute.

This first issue features Marjorie Alfonso, our coordinator in Curaçao, two recent posts from the blog, and an open invitation for members to suggest the next feature, write a post, or send something for the next issue.

— The DCDC Core Team

Member spotlight

Meet Marjorie Alfonso, coordinator in Curaçao

Marjorie Alfonso
Local Coordinator · Curaçao
University of Curaçao (Library & Research Services)

Marjorie is Information and AI Specialist at the University of Curaçao Library, supporting researchers and lecturers with research tools, digital resources, and emerging technologies. She believes researchers and students on small islands should have equal access to knowledge and the chance to make their work visible internationally. Her hope for the network is stronger collaboration between Caribbean islands and international partners, with more opportunities for training and knowledge sharing.
