The people behind the network

The DCDC Network is built by researchers, educators, and professionals who believe the Dutch Caribbean deserves better access to open science tools and knowledge.

Aruba

🙂
Rendell de Kort
Network Administrator & Facilitator — University of Aruba
What I work on I run an economics consultancy focused on sustainable and purpose-driven business models, and I'm completing a PhD on economic resilience in small island states. Data and how we use it sits at the center of most of what I do.
Why this matters to me The Dutch Caribbean produces real knowledge. But too much of it stays locked inside organizations, never shared, never built upon. That's a loss we can actually do something about.
What I'm hoping DCDC makes possible A researcher in Sint Maarten being able to build on work done in Aruba without starting from scratch. That kind of quiet, practical collaboration is what the region has been missing.
