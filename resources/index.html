Resources

Short, openly licensed guides from the DCDC Network on managing and sharing research data. Everything here is free to read, download, and reuse across the islands, with a persistent identifier so it stays findable and citable.

Two-pager

Sharing research data

A plain-language, two-page guide to sharing research data in the Dutch Caribbean: what is worth sharing, how to describe and license it, where to deposit it, and how a persistent identifier such as a DOI keeps your work findable and reusable long after the project ends. Written for anyone who works with data, no technical background assumed.
