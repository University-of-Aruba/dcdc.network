Data stewardship training (CaSDaR / DCC)

Online & free · weekly, Atlantic Standard Time

A six-part series on research data management, data ethics, FAIR data, and preservation, run by CaSDaR and the Digital Curation Centre. Most relevant for data stewards and for PhD researchers moving in that direction. Registration is on Eventbrite.

Open call

Dutch Data Prize 2026: nominations close

Research Data Netherlands (RDNL)

The Dutch Data Prize recognises researchers and teams who make their data Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable. This round is open to the Dutch Caribbean, teams as well as individuals, and you can nominate your own work. Each category winner receives €3,500 to further improve the FAIRness of their data, and this year accompanying code and software count too.
